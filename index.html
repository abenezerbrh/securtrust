About

Trained for what actually happens on site

Every SecurTrust guard goes through orientation training and testing before they're posted anywhere. That covers the Trespass to Property Act, the Private Investigator and Security Guard Act, arrest procedures, and self-defense, along with whatever the specific posting calls for, whether that's a film set at night or a retail floor on a busy weekend.

Supervisors check in on every posting to make sure our guards are meeting the standard we set, not just the minimum the job requires. That's the difference between a guard who's there to fill a shift and one who's actually watching.

We're fully insured, licensed, and available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across Toronto and the surrounding regions.

  • Orientation training & testing for every guard
  • Trespass to Property Act certified
  • Private Investigator and Security Guard Act certified
  • Arrest procedures & self-defense training
  • Active supervisor oversight on every posting
  • Fully insured, licensed operation
